DIY Lighting

by: Ester Penjam




Turn vintage lamps or light fixtures into one-of-a-kind pieces by painting them in a bold color or updating the lampshade with fabric or trim. You can also repurpose items like old colanders or wire baskets into pendant lights for a rustic touch.

  1. Pendant Lights with Personality:
  • Create your own pendant lights using materials like wire baskets, mason jars, or geometric shapes.
  • Customize the look with paint, fabric, or decorative accents to match your style and color scheme.
  • Hang them individually or in clusters to make a statement over a dining table, kitchen island, or entryway.
  1. Mason Jar Chandeliers:
  • Transform ordinary mason jars into rustic-chic chandeliers by attaching them to a wooden or metal frame.
  • Add LED candles or fairy lights inside the jars for a warm and inviting glow.
  • Hang the chandelier in a dining room, patio, or bedroom for a cozy and charming ambiance.
  1. Industrial Pipe Sconces:
  • Construct industrial-style sconces using metal pipes and fittings, paired with vintage-style Edison bulbs.
  • Mount them on a wall as bedside reading lights, hallway accents, or outdoor fixtures for an urban-inspired look.
  1. Rope Pendant Lamps:
  • Fashion pendant lamps using thick rope wrapped around a wire frame or glass globe
  • Experiment with different knotting techniques and rope colors to create unique and textural lighting fixtures.
  1. Wine Bottle Lamps:
  • Upcycle empty wine bottles into elegant table lamps by inserting a lamp kit and shade.
  • Paint or decorate the bottles for added flair, and choose LED bulbs for energy efficiency and safety.
  1. LED Light Strips:
  • Install LED light strips under cabinets, along shelves, or behind furniture to add ambient or task lighting.
  • Choose color-changing strips for mood lighting or stick to white for functional illumination.
  1. Share and Inspire:
  • Craft paper lanterns or origami lights using colorful paper and LED tea lights or string lights.
  • Hang them indoors or outdoors for festive decorations that can be easily customized for different occasions.
  1. Bottle Cap Lampshade:
  • Collect and clean bottle caps to create a quirky lampshade for a pendant light or table lamp.
  • Arrange the caps in a pattern or mosaic on a wire frame, and secure them with adhesive or wire for a unique lighting accent.
  1. Branch Chandelier:
  • Gather branches or driftwood and weave them into a natural chandelier frame.
  • Add string lights or candle holders to the branches for a rustic and organic lighting fixture.
  1. Terrarium Table Lamp:
  • Build a terrarium base using glass containers, succulents, and decorative rocks or sand.
  • Insert a lamp kit into the terrarium to create a stunning table lamp that brings nature indoors.
