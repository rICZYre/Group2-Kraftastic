Education

DIY education is a personalized approach to learning where individuals take control of their own educational journey outside of traditional institutions. It involves seeking out knowledge and skills independently through resources like online courses, tutorials, and hands-on projects. With the freedom to choose what, when, and how to learn, DIY education encourages curiosity, experimentation, and creativity. It empowers learners to explore diverse subjects, pursue passions, and develop practical skills relevant to their interests and goals.
